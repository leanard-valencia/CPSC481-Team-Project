Stage Two

We have decided to create a Home Repair and Services App for the team project for CPSC 481.

Stakeholders

For the second stage of the project we have identified the stakeholders of our app.

Customers will be the primary users of the app which can be mostly homeowners or tenants.

The service providers will also have access to the app in order to provide their services to the customers.

User Research

We used the following method cards for our user research:

  1. Historical Analysis (Learn)
  2. A Day in the Life (Look)
  3. Card Sort (Ask)
